Destination Guides

Aruba: The Caribbean Paradise

Turn your gaze toward Aruba‚Äîa small yet stunning island boasting some of the Caribbean's most beautiful beaches. Here's a deeper look at what awaits you in this island paradise!

Cultural Highlights

Aruba's culture is a vibrant tapestry woven from Dutch colonial heritage and a mix of Caribbean influences. The capital, Oranjestad, offers colorful architecture, rich arts, and a laid-back atmosphere.

Must-Visit Attractions

  1. Eagle Beach: Renowned for its soft white sands and calm, clear waters, it's a perfect spot for sunbathing or water sports.
  2. Arikok National Park: Explore hiking trails, unique geological formations, and local wildlife.
  3. Natural Pool: A hidden gem where you can swim in natural waters flanked by rugged cliffs.
  4. Local Markets: Experience vibrant local life at the central market, perfect for picking up crafts and souvenirs.

Culinary Delights

Don't miss out on tasting the local cuisine. Try Keshi Yena, a delicious cheese dish, or indulge in fresh seafood caught daily. Enjoy culinary experiences that celebrate the island's multicultural influences.

Practical Visitor Information

  • Getting Around: Rent a car for the best way to explore, as public transport can be limited.
  • Safety: Aruba is known for its safety and friendly locals, though standard travel precautions are always wise.
